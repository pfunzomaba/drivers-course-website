Learner's Licence R108.00

How it works:

It is important to note that we don't provide lessons and study materials for a learners licence test. We can help you to book your test date – E-natis online pre-booking. After that we inform you when your test date is, then on the day of the test we take you to the Testing Centre to write your test.

What is not included:

Two black and white or colour id photos and an optometrist eye test. Testing Centre Appointment Fee R108, and Issue Fee R60. Fees may change without notice. Testing Centre at Thohoyandou Sibasa do accept cash/credit payments. Please bring a bank card for a swipe transaction.

Note:

We usually book test dates at Thohoyandou Sibasa Testing Centres. The client must be present in person at the Testing Centre to confirm (pay) the booking. The approximate waiting period is 1-2 weeks for a test date depending on availability. The test is computer-based. If you study, you will pass.

Code 8 Limited + Driver's Licence Test R2000.00

How it works:

We are available 5 days per week from 08h00 to 18h00 for training per appointment. We only collect and drop off students who use the same route to their homes as the driver(from Tshisaulu to Thohoyandou). If not you have to make your own transport arrangements.

Note:

As this is a limited lesson, you have two months to train and pass your drivers licence test. Driver's Licence Test cost is included.

Driver's Licence Test: Truck/Car for Hire R500.00

How it works:

To start – Fill in the client registration form. You have to go on your own to Thohoyandou Sibasa Testing center and book your test. On the day of your test we take you to the Testing Centre to do the test with our car.

What is not included:

Two black and white or colour id photos and optometrist eye test. Testing Centre Appointment Fee R228, and Issue Fee R228. Fees may change without notice. Testing Centre at Thohoyandou Sibasa do accept cash/credit payments. Please bring a bank card for a swipe transaction.

Note:

We are registered at Thohoyandou Sibasa Testing center only, so please book your test at Thohoyandou Sibasa DLTC.
